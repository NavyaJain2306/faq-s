FAQ

Who can participate in Tech Week? ⌄
Tech Week is open to all students, irrespective of branch or year. Some events may be beginner-friendly, while others may require basic technical knowledge.
Is Tech Week only for women? ⌄
No. Although ACM-W focuses on supporting women in technology, Tech Week events are open to everyone unless explicitly mentioned.
Is there any registration fee? ⌄
Most events are free of cost. If an event requires a registration fee, it will be clearly mentioned in the event details.
How do I register for events? ⌄
Registration links will be provided on the Events page of the website. Each event may have a separate registration form.
Will I receive a certificate? ⌄
Certificates of participation will be provided for selected events. Details will be communicated during the event.
